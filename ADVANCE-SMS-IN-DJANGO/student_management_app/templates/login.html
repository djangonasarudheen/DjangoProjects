Welcome to Benchmarks School

Benchmarks International School is one of the leading CBSE affiliated schools in Malappuram Dist(Affiliation No: 931189). of Kerala where the children find learning a joyful experience. The school has classes from Montessori up to Grade 10. The special facilities provided by the school along with the intervention of qualified and experienced teachers ensure a holistic development of the children. As the name suggests, the school aims to create a ‘benchmark’ for all similar institutions in terms of quality, and excellence in holistic education.
